Cossidae

The family, of cosmopolitan distribution, comprises about 1000 described species. Most of these occur in the tropics, but many are found in the Holarctic region (Scoble 1992: 260).
Identification: Baker 1985, Forster & Wohlfahrt 1960, Hoffmeyer 1974, Koch 1964, Laštuvka & Laštuvka 1995, Schnaider et al. 1961, Skinner 1984, Spatenka et al. 1993, 1999, Sukhareva 1989d.
