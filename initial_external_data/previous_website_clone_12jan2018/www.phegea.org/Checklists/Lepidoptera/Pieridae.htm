Pieridae are represented in all main zoogeographical regions, with about 1200 described species (Scoble 1992: 309). Well over 40 species occur in the West-Palaearctic.
Identification: Bink 1992, Ebert & Rennwald 1991, Emmet & Heath 1989, Forster & Wohlfahrt 1955, Hesselbarth, van Oorschot & Wagener 1995, Higgins & Hargreaves 1983, Higgins & Riley 1970 (+ later editions, reprints and traductions in several languages), Howarth 1973, Krzywicki 1962, Niculescu 1963, Seitz 1906-1909, Whalley 1981, Wynhoff, Made & Swaay 1990.
