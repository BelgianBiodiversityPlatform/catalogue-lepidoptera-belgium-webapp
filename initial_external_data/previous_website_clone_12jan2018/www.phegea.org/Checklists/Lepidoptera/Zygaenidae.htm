Sesiidae

Zygaenidae includes about 800 described species. They are represented in all zoogeographical regions. Most species occur in the tropics, but the family is well represented in the Palaearctic region (Scoble 1992: 264).
Identification: Dabrowski 1965, Efetov & Tarmann 1999, Forster & Wohlfahrt 1960, Hoffmeyer 1974, Koch 1964, Naumann et al. 1999, Racheli 1990, Skinner 1984, Sukhareva 1989c, Tremewan 1985.
Nomenclature according to Fauna Europaea.
