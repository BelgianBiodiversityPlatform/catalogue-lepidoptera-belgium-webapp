Eudemis profundana ([Denis & Schiffermüller], 1775)

A rather rare species in Belgium.

The larva lives in a leaf of Quercus rolled round the midrib. Pupation takes places inside the larval habitation or amongst leaf-litter on the ground.

The adults fly from mid June towards August. They are active from evening onwards and later come to light.

Belgium, Namur, Ave-et-Auffe, 07 July 2006.
