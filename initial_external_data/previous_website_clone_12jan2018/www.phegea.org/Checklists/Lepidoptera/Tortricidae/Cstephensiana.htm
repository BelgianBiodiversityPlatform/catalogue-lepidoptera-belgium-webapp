Cnephasia stephensiana (Doubleday, 1849)

A rather common species throughout Belgium.

The larva lives on various herbaceous plant species. It does not feed before hibernating. After hibernation, it first mines a leaf, then lives between leaves spun together. It pupates in the larval habitation, or spun up nearby on the plant.

The adults have been observed from mid May till till August.

Belgium, Namur, Lessive, 15 May 2004.
