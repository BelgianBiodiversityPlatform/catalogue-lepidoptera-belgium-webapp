Thyrididae

Pterophoridae are represented in all the main zoogeographical regions, with about 1100 species (Gielis 1993: 28).
Identification: Adamczewski 1951, Arenberger 1977, 1995, 2002, Beirne 1952, Bigot 1960, 1961, 1962, 1963a, b, 1964, 1966, Buszko 1979, 1986, Gibeaux & Nel 1991, Gielis 1996, Gozmány 1963, Hannemann 1977, Janmoulle 1959, Kuchlein & Gielis 1982, Razowski 1988, van Deurs 1948, Zagulyaev 1997a.
