Depressariidae
Elachistidae

Depressariidae are often treated as a subfamily of Oecophoridae, a family which is not monophyletic. It is a cosmopolitan group particularly well represented in the temperate areas (Scoble 1992: 243).
Identification: Gozmány 1955a, 1958, Hannemann 1953, 1995, Harper et al. 2002, Jacobs 1949, 1950, 1955, Lvovsky 1990, Palm 1973, 1975, 1978, 1989, Toll 1964, van Laar 1961, 1964.
Nomenclature according to Fauna Europaea.
Species in Belgium: 46.
