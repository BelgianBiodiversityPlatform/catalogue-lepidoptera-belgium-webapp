The Glyphipterigidae have a world-wide distribution. In the Palaearctic region the family is mainly represented by the large genus Glyphipterix, with 54 species (Diakonoff 1986: 250).
Identification: Buszko & Baraniak 1985, Diakonoff 1986, Ford 1950, 1953, Gozmány 1955a, 1956, Kuznetzov 1990b, Palm 1974, Pelham-Clinton 1985, Toll 1956.
Nomenclature according to Fauna Europaea.
