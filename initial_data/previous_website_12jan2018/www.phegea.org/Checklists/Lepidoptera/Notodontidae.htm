Thaumetopoeidae
Notodontidae
Noctuidae

The Notodontidae include about 3000 species and is distributed in all major zoogeographical regions. Pygaerinae include only the genus Clostera with 43 described species in all the main zoogeographical regions. The composition of the Notodontinae, with at least 200 species, the Phalerinae and the Heterocampinae remains uncertain (Scoble 1992: 331).
Identification: de Freina & Witt 1987, Forster & Wohlfahrt 1960, Hoffmeyer 1974, Koch 1964, Skinner 1984, Soltys 1965.
