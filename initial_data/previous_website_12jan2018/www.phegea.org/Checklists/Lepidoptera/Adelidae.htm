Species of this family are characterised by their particularly long antennae. The family has a world-wide distribution (Scoble 1992: 214).
Identification: Benander 1953, Gozmány 1965, Heath & Pelham-Clinton 1976, Jacobs 1948, Küppers 1980, Nielsen 1985, Razowski 1978, Wojtusiak 1972, Zagulyaev 1989g.
Nomenclature according to Fauna Europaea.
