Philedone gerningana ([Denis & Schiffermüller], 1775)

A very rare species in Belgium.

The larva feeds polyphagously in leaves of Lotus, Plantago, Scabiosa, Potentilla, Vaccinium and tress like Populus tremula, Abies etc. Pupates in a long elongate white shelter in the larval feeding place.

The adults fly in one generation: from late June towards mid August.

Belgium, Namur, Nismes, 21 August 2015.
