Acherontia atropos (Linnaeus, 1758)

A very rare migrant in Belgium, arriving in late summer.

The larva lives on different species of Solanum, but mainly on Solanum tuberosum. Pupates in a loose cocoon in the ground.

Most adults have been observed in summertime. They come to light, and have the rare habit of entering bee hives.

Belgium, Limburg, Kinrooi, 30 September 2005.
