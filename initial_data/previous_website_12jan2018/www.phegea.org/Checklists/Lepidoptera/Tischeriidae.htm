Tischeriidae is a primarily Holarctic family. Most of the species (about 70 species are described) occur in North America. The family is absent from Australasia (Scoble 1992: 223).
Identification: Benander 1953, Brown 1953, Emmet 1976c, Gozmány 1965, Toll 1959b, Zagulyaev 1989e.
Nomenclature according to Fauna Europaea.
