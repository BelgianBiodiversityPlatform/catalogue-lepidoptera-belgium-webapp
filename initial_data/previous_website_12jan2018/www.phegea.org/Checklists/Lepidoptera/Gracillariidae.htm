Gracillariidae
Yponomeutidae

The cosmopolitan family Gracillariidae includes over 1600 species. The larvae of most species are leaf miners, at least in their early stages. The genus Phyllocnistis is included, though it was often considered a separate family in the past (Scoble 1992: 231).
Identification: Benander 1945a, Bradley et al. 1969, Brown 1947, Buszko 1981b, Emmet 1985, Emmet et al. 1985, Gozmány 1956, Gregor 1952, Jacobs 1945, Janmoulle 1962b, Kuznetzov 1990a, Lempke 1976a, Patzak 1986, Seksyaeva 1990b, Szöcs 1956.
Nomenclature according to Fauna Europaea, except for Caloptilia semifascia which is considered to be a synonym of Caloptilia onustella .
Species in Belgium: 102.
