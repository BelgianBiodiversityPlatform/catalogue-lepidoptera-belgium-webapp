The Crambiform group of the former family Pyralidae s.l. is composed of Crambinae, Schoenobiinae, Scopariinae, Nymphulinae, Evergestinae, Odontiinae, Heliothinae and Pyraustinae.
Identification: Bleszynski 1956, 1957, 1965a, De Prins 1977, Falkovitch 1997, Gaedike 1980, Gaedike & Petersen 1985, Goater 1986, Gozmány 1963, Hannemann 1964, Janmoulle 1959, 1962a, Kuchlein 1978, Kuchlein & Gielis 1982, Kuchlein, Leffef & Kleinpaste 1980, Marion 1954-1957, 1979-1976, Martin 1997, Opheim 1963, Palm 1986, Petersen 1973, Petersen, Friese & Rinnhofer 1973, Petersen & Gaedike 1980, Rinnhofer 1975, 1980, Roesler 1966, 1973, 1993, Schwarz 1963, Sinev 1986, Slamka 1995, 1997, Soffner 1956, Speidel 1984, van Deurs 1942, Whalley & Tweedie 1963.
