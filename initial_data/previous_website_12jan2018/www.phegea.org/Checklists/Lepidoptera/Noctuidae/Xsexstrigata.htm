Xestia sexstrigata (Haworth, 1809)

A not so common and local species throughout Belgium.

The larva lives on different herbaceous plants like Galium saxatile, Plantago lanceolata, Rubus, Scilla non-scripta and Scrophularia. Hibernates as a larva and pupates underground.

The adults fly in one generation a year; from mid July till mid September. They come to light and sugar.

Belgium, Antwerpen, Brecht, 27 August 2003.
