Eriocraniidae are confined to the Holarctic region with less than 30 species described (Scoble 1992: 199).
Identification: Benander 1953, Gozmány 1965, Heath 1958, 1976b, Razowski 1975, Toll 1959a, Viette 1948, Zagulyaev 1989b.
