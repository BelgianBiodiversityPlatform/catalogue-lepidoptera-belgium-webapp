Nepticulidae
Opostegidae

The Nepticulidae, cosmopolitan in distribution, comprise about 700 described species but probably many undescribed (van Nieukerken 1986, 1997 pers. comm.). They include the smallest Lepidoptera with an average wing span of 5-6 mm, in some species it is under 4 mm. The larvae of most species mine leaves (Scoble 1992: 219).
Identification: Benander 1953, Emmet 1976a, Johansson et al. 1990, van Nieukerken 1985, Puplesis 1994, Szöcs 1965, Zagulyaev 1989c.
Nomenclature according to Fauna Europaea.
Species in Belgium: 81.
