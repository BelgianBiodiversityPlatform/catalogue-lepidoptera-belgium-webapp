There are more than 4000 species described in this cosmopolitan family (Scoble 1992: 250).
Identification: Amsel 1935, Bland et al. 2002, Elsner et al. 1999, Englert 1974, Gozmány 1955b, 1957, 1958, Huemer 1988, Huemer & Karsholt 1999, Karsholt 1981, Klimesch 1953, 1954, Piskunov 1990b, Pitkin 1984, 1988, Povolný 1966, 1967, 1983, 2002, Sattler 1960a, 1960b, 1962, 1968, 1976, 1986, Sokoloff 1985, Svensson 1962, Wolff 1958.
